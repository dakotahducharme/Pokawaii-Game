Welcome to Pokawaii! The cutest version of all of your favorite games mixed together! The objective of the game is to collect all of the items on the screen for each character! Once you've collected all of the items, you might attract a Pokemon! Press start to begin, and then choose your character.
Use your arrow keys or WASD keys to move your character around
